Bio

Erin Goins is a senior at Virginia Tech and will soon graduate with a degree in Creative Writing (hopefully.) She is the author of many incredible short stories that have yet to see the light of day and is the Kanye West of her generation. She genuinely loves film, theatre, acting, camera work, and anything to do with the cinematic experience. Naturally, then, she was hype to be part of a group of revolutionary, awe-inspiring film critics called "The Movie Manifesto", and this site will exhibit some of the work they produced.

The Future in Film

One thing about film: in order for one to be a success, every facet of it must work -- the director must be poignant, the actors must have range, even the audience needs to generate enough of a reaction to make it all come together seamlessly. The Movie Manifesto focuses an every aspect of film, and you can too by clicking here to learn how to really analyze a movie.
