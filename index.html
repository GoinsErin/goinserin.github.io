Bio

Erin Goins is a senior at Virginia Tech and will soon graduate with a degree in Creative Writing (hopefully.) She is the author of many incredible short stories that have yet to see the light of day and is the Kanye West of her generation. She genuinely loves film, theatre, acting, camera work, and anything to do with the cinematic experience. Naturally, then, she was hype to be part of a group of revolutionary, awe-inspiring film critics called "The Movie Manifesto", and this site will exhibit some of the work they produced. (Editor's Note: replace "many" with "four", "incredible" with "mediocre", and "Kanye West" with like "Rob Schneider" or someone. )
